How To Play:

Play as the mouse to reach your cheese before the cat reaches his milk. Unlock the correct doors with a special key to find your special treat. Be careful! The mischievous cat might be hiding behind the door! If he catches you, he will steal the key to unlock the doors to his milk. Take back the key by hiding behind the door you think he will pick.
